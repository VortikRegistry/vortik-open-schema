# Vortik Research Documents

This section contains extended research artifacts connected to the **Vortik Semantic Registry**.

These documents explore structural, semantic, and coordination dynamics across Ethereum protocol research, infrastructure, and ENS-anchored semantic surfaces.

They are interpretive research documents.

They are not Ethereum protocol specifications.

## Purpose

These documents are research artifacts designed to:

- map emerging coordination structures
- track semantic stabilization
- support alignment between ENS naming and technical terminology
- distinguish protocol-native concepts from external or deprecated abstractions
- provide interpretive context around registry anchors

They do not define protocol rules.

They do not replace EIPs, specifications, schemas, anchors, or registry entries.
