# epbs.eth

Semantic anchor representing **Enshrined Proposer-Builder Separation (ePBS)**.

## Context

Proposer-Builder Separation (PBS) emerged as a mechanism to separate block construction from block proposal in order to mitigate MEV concentration and improve validator neutrality.

Enshrined Proposer-Builder Separation (ePBS) proposes integrating this separation directly into the Ethereum protocol.

The primary specification for this research direction is **EIP-7732**.

By moving PBS into the protocol layer, ePBS aims to:

- improve censorship resistance  
- reduce centralization risks in the MEV supply chain  
- simplify block production architecture

## Registry Role

- Track terminology convergence around ePBS
- Document the conceptual transition from off-protocol PBS to enshrined PBS
- Distinguish protocol-level PBS from external builder market infrastructure
