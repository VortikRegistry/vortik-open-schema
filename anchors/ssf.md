# SSF (Single Slot Finality)

Semantic anchor representing **Single Slot Finality (SSF)** research in Ethereum consensus.

## Context

Single Slot Finality (SSF) is a proposed improvement to Ethereum consensus allowing blocks to reach finality within a single slot.

This would significantly reduce finalization latency compared to the current Casper FFG model.

SSF is actively discussed in Ethereum research as a long-term evolution of the beacon chain consensus layer.

## Registry Role

This anchor tracks terminology stabilization and conceptual convergence related to SSF research.

The registry documents the semantic surface associated with Single Slot Finality discussions.
