## Summary

This anchor tracks **proving markets** as an external coordination surface for proof generation across zk systems, rollups, validity proving, and distributed proving infrastructure.

Proving markets describe environments where provers, proof networks, and proof-generation services coordinate around the production, pricing, verification, and delivery of cryptographic proofs.

This surface is relevant to Ethereum-adjacent scaling systems, but it is not currently a canonical Ethereum L1 protocol primitive.

---

## Context

As zk systems and rollup architectures grow, proof generation becomes a major coordination problem.

Different systems may require:

- fast proof generation  
- distributed proving networks  
- proof aggregation  
- proof marketplaces  
- specialized prover hardware  
- coordination between sequencers, provers, rollups, and settlement layers  

The term **proving markets** captures this emerging economic and technical coordination surface.

However, the term remains external to the core Ethereum L1 protocol ontology.
