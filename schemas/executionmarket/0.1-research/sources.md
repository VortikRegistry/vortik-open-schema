# Execution Market — Sources

## Research Context

Execution markets describe coordination layers where transactions, intents and liquidity sources interact to determine execution outcomes.

These markets often involve solvers, builders and routing systems interacting through auction mechanisms, routing systems or solver-based competition.

Execution markets typically emerge at the intersection of:

- intent-based architectures
- solver networks
- execution routing systems
- block building infrastructure
