# Contributing to Vortik Semantic Registry

Thank you for your interest in improving the Vortik Semantic Registry.

This repository tracks terminology stabilization and semantic anchors
across emerging Ethereum L1 coordination primitives.

## Contribution Principles

Vortik operates as a **research-first registry**. Contributions should:

- Reference **primary sources** whenever possible
- Avoid speculative or marketing terminology
- Focus on **terminology stabilization**
- Preserve minimal schema design

This repository **does not define protocol rules**.

## Reporting Terminology Drift

If you observe terminology divergence across research discussions,
please open an Issue including:

- Source links
- Observed terminology
- Suggested canonical term

## Proposing New Anchors

When proposing a new anchor, please include:

- Clear protocol primitive surface
- At least 2 primary research references
- Evidence of terminology convergence

Anchors should represent **coordination primitives**, not products or projects.

## Updating Sources

Sources should prioritize:

- Ethereum Research forum
- EIPs
- Core developer discussions
- Primary technical blog posts

Avoid secondary commentary whenever possible.

## Scope Reminder

Vortik documents **semantic surfaces**, not implementations.

The goal is to help track conceptual convergence across Ethereum L1 coordination layers.
